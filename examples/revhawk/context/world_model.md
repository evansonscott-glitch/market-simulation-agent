### Reasons for Customer Churn

Customer churn in the pest control industry is often preventable and stems from a few key areas:

*   **Seasonal Needs and Misaligned Expectations:** Customers may perceive pest control as a seasonal need and discontinue service once an immediate issue is resolved. [4]
*   **Financial Concerns:** Economic uncertainty can lead customers to cut back on services they deem non-essential. [4]
*   **Service Quality or Unresolved Issues:** Recurring pest problems or poor customer service experiences are significant drivers of churn. [4]
*   **Lack of Communication or Follow-Up:** Inadequate communication, such as a lack of service reminders or follow-ups, can make customers feel undervalued. [4]
*   **Competition and DIY Options:** The availability of lower-priced competitors and DIY products presents ongoing challenges to customer retention. [4]
